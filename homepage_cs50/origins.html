Hello Again! My Country is

Rwanda

Rwanda is a small country in East-Africa, about the size of Maryland. However, do not let it's size deceive you! It is one of the fastest growing economies in East-Africa, and is quickly becoming the bussiness/tech hub of the region. In all my years, the beauty of Rwanda is unmatched; it is called the land of a thousand hills, home to the last remaining silverbacks, the source of the Nile, and so much more. Growing up there was truly an amazing experience! Visit Rwanda and see for yourself!
